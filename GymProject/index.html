Welcome To O2XYGEN
Oxygen Gym is a co-ed fitness center of over 1000 square meters, offering hi-tech weight training, boxing, and cardio equipment. It is one of the largest co-ed gyms, offering 24/7 flexibility to work out at any time that suits your schedule. The gym is dedicated to helping individuals achieve their fitness goals, improve their health, and reduce stress. It is open to both early and late-night customers.
